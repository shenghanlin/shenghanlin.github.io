Hello! I’m a Deep Learning Researcher and Competitor with over 2 years experience, particularly in Computer Vision and Audio. My driving force? Making this world a better place with innovation of AI.

Just like a detective, I find joy in dissecting complex research problems and crafting solutions through code, and math. My perseverance has led to the authorship of 5 papers and co-authorship of 2 more. Beyond academia, I thrive in competitions like Kaggle, DrivenData, etc. It’s here that I became one of the youngest Kaggle Grandmasters, peaking at 5th (now 7th) out of 285K participants. I also take pride in open-source contributing on GitHub, in platforms such as TensorFlow, Keras, HuggingFace, YOLOv5, etc.
